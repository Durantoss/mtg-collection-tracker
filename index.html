MTG Rules Reference

Basic Rules

Turn Structure

Each turn consists of five phases: Beginning, Main, Combat, Main, and End.

Mana System

Mana is the resource used to cast spells. It comes in five colors plus colorless.

Combat Rules

Combat Phases

Combat consists of: Beginning of Combat, Declare Attackers, Declare Blockers, Combat Damage, End of Combat.

Damage Assignment

Attacking creatures deal damage equal to their power to defending player or planeswalker.
